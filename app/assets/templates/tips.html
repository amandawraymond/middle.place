But Wait there's more...

General

  • When generating a Shareable Link, put your address second so that the recipient can use Geolocation
  • Add a Shareable Link to a calendar invite, asking the recipient to pick a place
  • Trading something off of Craigslist? Input an address that's *near* your house (don't use your home address for
    • safety reasons) and send it to them

Social

  • Use geolocation and explore places near you
  • Have friends who live 20 minutes to 1 hour away? Find a midpoint and reconnect
  • Going on a sweet road trip? Find a hotel or food at a good halfway point

Professionals

  • Append your email signatures with a link to your saved work address and call it "Meet with me for lunch"
  • Add a shareable link to your LinkedIn profile with your work address
  • If you're moving to a new place, input your work addresses to find an apartment/house in the middle
  • Put it on your personal site to meet with local clients and recruiters
  • Working on a project with some peers, colleagues, or friends? Find a good spot for wifi
  • Planning a conference or tournament is stressful. Use Middle.Place to find nearby hotels and food
